User Manual

Welcome to the HK Smart Price Tracker! Our system syncs daily with government data to help you compare prices across major retail chains in Hong Kong.

🔍 Core Features & Search Tips

  • Smart Search: Type a brand or product name in the chat box to find items.
  • Discount Filter: Use the "Discounted Only" chip to show products on sale.
  • Unit Price Calculation: Automatically calculates the effective average price for promotions like Buy 1 Get 1 Free.

📱 How to Add to Home Screen

Android Devices: Open in Chrome ➔ Tap top-right menu "⋮" ➔ Add to Home screen.
iOS Devices: Open in Safari ➔ Tap bottom "Share" icon ➔ Add to Home Screen.
