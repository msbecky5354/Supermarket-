User Manual

Welcome to HK Smart Price! Our system automatically syncs with the government's data daily, helping you easily compare the latest prices across major supermarkets in Hong Kong.

🔍 Core Features & Search Tips

  • Smart Chat Search: Enter a "Brand" or "Product Name" on the home page to instantly view supermarket pricing and discount details straight from the official dataset.
  • One-Click Discounts: Use the "Latest Deals" button on the home page to view only products with ongoing promotions.
  • 🔥 Catch Huge Price Gaps: Tap the "🔥 Big Price Gap" button. The system filters products with a price difference of over 10% between stores, helping you spot the best bargains instantly!
  • Smart Unit Price: Automatically calculates the effective unit price (indicated by 💰) for complex promotions like "Buy 2 for $X" or "2nd item half price".

🔔 Target Price Alert

Waiting for a price drop? Tap the orange bell 🔔 on any product card and enter your target price to track it.

  • Daily Auto-Check: Every time you open the app, it automatically matches the latest prices against your target.
  • Instant Notification: When the price drops to or below your target, the 🔔 at the top of the screen will light up with a red dot!
🛡️

Privacy Protection: Your watchlist is stored solely on your device's browser. We do not collect your phone number or email, ensuring zero personal data tracking.

💡 Why can't I find certain products?

Our data links directly to the government and Consumer Council's "Online Price Watch". To ensure pricing accuracy and stable daily updates, the system mainly monitors around 3,000 of the most commonly purchased "daily essentials".

Therefore, highly niche, limited-time, or single-store exclusive products might not be included in our database. We recommend using simple "Brand Names" or "Keywords" for your searches!

📱 How to Add to Home Screen

Android: Open in Chrome ➔ Tap "⋮" on the top right ➔ Select "Add to Home screen".
iOS: Open in Safari ➔ Tap the "Share" icon at the bottom ➔ Select "Add to Home Screen".
