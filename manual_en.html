User Manual

Welcome to "HK Smart Price"! Our system automatically syncs with the government's "Price Watch" data to help you easily compare the latest prices across major HK supermarket chains.

🔍 Core Features & Search Tips

  • Conversational Search: Type any "brand" or "product name" on the homepage for instant pricing and promotional details.
  • One-Click Deals: Use the "Latest Deals" button to filter and show only discounted items.
  • 🔥 Big Price Gaps: Click "🔥 Big Price Gap" to find items with over 10% price differences between supermarkets.
  • Unit Price Calculation: System automatically calculates "buy 2" or "half-price" deals for you, marked with 💰.
  • Quick Navigation: Want to go home? Just type "Back to menu" or "Home" in the chat box to navigate back instantly.

📸 AI Visual Search

Snap a photo of the product, and our AI will identify it and fill in the search box for you. Input your Gemini API Key for higher accuracy!

Note: Without an API Key, the system defaults to a lightweight offline recognition mode.

🔔 Price Alert Feature

Waiting for a sale? Tap the orange bell icon 🔔 on any product card, set your target price, and track it. When the price drops below your target, the 🔔 at the top of the app will glow red!

🛡️ Disclaimer & Privacy Policy

1. Privacy: This App has no user accounts. All "Price Alerts" and "Search History" are stored locally in your device's browser only. We do not track, monitor, or sell your personal data.

2. Data Source: Price data is sourced from the "Price Watch" dataset by Data.gov.hk. Copyright belongs to the Consumer Council and respective merchants.

3. Disclaimer: This is a free open-source tool. Information is for reference only. Actual prices may vary; please verify with store tags. We are not liable for any losses due to pricing inaccuracies.
