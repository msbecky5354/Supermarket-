User Manual

Welcome to "SaveMore🔎"! Our platform automatically synchronizes daily supermarket data from the government's "Online Price Watch", helping you effortlessly compare prices across major supermarkets and retail chains in Hong Kong.

🔍 Core Features & Search Tips

  • Smart Interactive Search: Select a main category and type the brand name or product keyword directly to locate your items.
  • One-Click Promotions Filter: Tap the "Offers Only" button at the top to display only items with active promotional events.
  • 🔥 Spot Huge Price Gaps: Tap the "🔥 Price Gap" shortcut button at the bottom. The system will instantly filter out items with a price variance of over 10% between different retailers, exposing overpriced stores and highlighting the best bargains!
  • Smart Unit Price Conversion: Automatically calculates the net unit price for complex promotional offers like "Buy 1 Get 1 Free".

🔔 New Feature: Smart Price Alert

Found an item but waiting for a better price? Now you can track it! Simply tap the Orange Bell icon 🔔 on any product card, enter your target price, and add it to your watchlist.

  • Daily Auto-Check: Every time you open the app, it automatically cross-checks the latest prices for you.
  • Instant Red Badge Notification: When a tracked item falls below your target price, the Rose Red Bell icon 🔔 at the top of the screen will light up with a red dot!
🛡️

Ultimate Privacy Protection: This feature runs entirely on offline client-side technology. Your watchlist is stored exclusively inside your own mobile browser. We do not collect or store your phone number or email—zero personal data, zero tracking ads!

💡 Why can't I find certain products?

Our database pulls data directly from the Consumer Council's "Online Price Watch". To maintain absolute accuracy and stability, the system focuses on tracking approximately 3,000 daily essential commodities most frequently purchased by citizens.

Consequently, highly niche products, limited edition items, or exclusive supermarket brands may not be covered. We highly recommend searching with broader "Brand Names" or simple keywords!

📱 How to Add to Home Screen

Android Devices: Open in Chrome ➔ Tap the "⋮" menu in the top right ➔ Select "Add to Home screen".
iOS Devices: Open in Safari ➔ Tap the "Share" icon at the bottom ➔ Select "Add to Home Screen".
