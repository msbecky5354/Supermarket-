### 簡介
歡迎使用「香港生活百貨價格神器」！本系統每日自動同步政府「網上價格一覽通」數據，助你輕鬆比較全港各大超市及連鎖商戶（包含個人護理店等）的生活百貨價格，找出全城最抵買的貨品。

### 核心功能與搜尋技巧
* **🔍 智能對話式搜尋：** 點選主分類後，可直接於下方輸入框輸入「品牌」或「貨品名稱」關鍵字，系統會即時為你找出相關貨品。
* **🎯 一鍵篩選優惠：** 善用對話框上方的快捷鍵（如「淨睇優惠」），系統會自動過濾，只顯示目前有減價或促銷活動的貨品，幫你慳到盡。
* **🧮 智能換算單價：** 自動計算各種複雜優惠（如買一送一、第2件半價）的折合單件平均價，並標示最低價商戶，邊間最抵一目了然。
* **🔗 輕鬆分享情報：** 每張貨品卡片均設有專屬分享按鈕，一鍵即可將最抵價格情報複製或透過 WhatsApp 傳送給親朋好友。
* **🔄 每日數據更新：** 數據每日定時自動同步，確保為最新價格。

### 如何將本程式加入手機主螢幕（免安裝 App）
你可以將本網站直接加入手機桌面，像原生 App 一樣全螢幕開啟及使用：

1. **Android 系統：**
   * 使用 Chrome 瀏覽器開啟本網站。
   * 點擊瀏覽器右上角的「三個點」選單圖示。
   * 選擇「加至主畫面」（Add to Home screen）。
   * 點擊「新增」，圖示便會出現在你的手機桌面上。

2. **iOS 系統：**
   * 使用 Safari 瀏覽器開啟本網站。
   * 點擊螢幕底部的「分享」圖示（一個向上指的箭咀）。
   * 向下滑動選單，選擇「加入主畫面」（Add to Home Screen）。
   * 點擊右上角的「加入」，即可在手機桌面找到應用程式圖示。

## 🇬🇧 English Version

### Introduction
Welcome to the HK Smart Price Tracker! Our system automatically syncs daily with the government's "Online Price Watch" data, helping you easily compare grocery prices across major supermarkets and retail chains (including personal care stores) to find the best deals in town.

### Core Features & Search Tips
* **🔍 Smart Chat Search:** After selecting a main category, type a "brand" or "product name" keyword in the chat box below. The system will instantly fetch relevant items for you.
* **🎯 One-Click Discount Filter:** Use the quick action chips (e.g., "Discounted Only") above the chat box to filter and display only products currently on sale or with promotional offers.
* **🧮 Smart Unit Price Calculation:** Automatically calculates the effective average price per item for complex promotions (e.g., Buy 1 Get 1 Free, 50% off the 2nd item) and highlights the store with the lowest price.
* **🔗 Easy Deal Sharing:** Each product card features a dedicated share button, allowing you to copy the link or share the best price instantly with family and friends via WhatsApp.
* **🔄 Daily Updates:** Data is synchronized automatically every day to ensure you always get the latest prices.

### How to Add to Your Home Screen (No App Store Required)
You can add this website directly to your phone's home screen and use it just like a native full-screen app:

1. **Android Devices:**
   * Open this website using the Chrome browser.
   * Tap the "three dots" menu icon in the top right corner.
   * Select "Add to Home screen".
   * Tap "Add," and the app icon will appear on your home screen.

2. **iOS Devices:**
   * Open this website using the Safari browser.
   * Tap the "Share" icon at the bottom of the screen (a square with an upward arrow).
   * Scroll down the menu and select "Add to Home Screen".
   * Tap "Add" in the top right corner, and you will find the app icon on your phone home screen.
